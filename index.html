Privacy Policy for Ariana

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Ariana ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Ariana Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

A. User-Provided Content

  • Voice Data: When you use the dictation feature, audio is processed temporarily to convert speech to text. We do not store raw audio recordings on our servers.
  • Script Content: The text you dictate, generate, or refine is sent to our backend features solely for the purpose of processing (e.g., generating titles or refining grammar).

B. Local Data

  • API Keys: If you choose to use the "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) feature, your Google Gemini or OpenAI API keys are stored locally on your device using Chrome Storage. We do not have access to these keys.
  • Settings: Preferences such as teleprompter speed and font size are stored locally.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information for the Single Purpose of facilitating script creation:

  • To convert speech to text.
  • To refine and structure text using AI models.
  • To generate metadata (titles, chapters) for your content.

3. Third-Party Data Processors

We share data with specific third-party providers only as necessary to provide the service:

  • Supabase: Used as our secure backend infrastructure and authentication provider. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini (Google Cloud): Used for AI text processing and generation. Google Cloud Privacy

4. Data Retention and Security

  • No Data Selling: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.
  • Encryption: All data transmission between the extension and our servers occurs over secure SSL/TLS encrypted connections.

5. Your Rights

You may delete the extension at any time to remove all local data. For account deletion requests regarding the authentication system, please contact support.
